Hollow Knight - Hallownest

Explore the mysterious world of Hallownest and its characters!

The Knight

The Knight is the silent protagonist of Hollow Knight, traveling through Hallownest to uncover its secrets. Armed with a nail, the Knight faces many challenges and mysterious enemies.

Hornet

Hornet is a skilled warrior and protector of Hallownest. She appears several times to challenge the Knight and guide them through important trials. Hornet is fast and agile, wielding a needle as her weapon.

Quirrel

Quirrel is a friendly explorer who helps the Knight along their journey. He is curious and insightful, often providing hints about the history and mysteries of Hallownest.
